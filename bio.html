FATAL's Biography


Shenic Tissera aka FATAL, residing in Negombo, is one of the best and well known upcoming bedroom producers throughout Sri Lanka. Starting off as a DJ at the age of sixteen, FATAL acquired a lot of recognition, experience and network in the local EDM scene. With the help of friends and family, he pursued his passion for producing at the age of seventeen; and seized it onto greater levels.


FATAL and his exceptional skill of producing various kinds of Electronic genres, which includes - Electronic, Trap, House, Electro, Cinematic and other; has turned a lot of heads. This had helped him with a lot of opportunities to collaborate with many local and foreign producers alongside further international recognition.


Breaking big, FATAL's livelihood as a producer, kick started when he was signed onto by SXKY Records; a local EDM Record Label. He has also been featured on the YES FM's Home Grown Top 15 hosted by Yazmin Yousuf and team. International record label Paradoxium taking keen interest on his work, released his profound Future Bass track "Right Here" and the amazing progressive track "A Better Way". Other promotional channels and labels such as Gallery of Music/Gallery of Music Trap have shown continuous support.


FATAL unleashed his biggest work yet with the release of his newest EP "New Chapters Available" and also has recently signed in collaboration with team High School Junkies ; to score the upcoming short film "EIDETIC", a Sri Lankan action thriller that will be screened at the Raindance Fest in 2017.


FATAL has released bounteous tracks of various kinds of genres and they are available on many international platforms such as Soundcloud, Youtube, The Artist Union; and is now available on Soundgimmicks.
